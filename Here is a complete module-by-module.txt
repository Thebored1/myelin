## Module 1 — Worker Profile & Records Management

The central registry of every individual who works at, visits, or is engaged with the organization. Every other module traces back to this record as the single source of truth.

**Personal & Identity Data**
- Full legal name, date of birth, nationality, and gender
- Government-issued ID upload and verification (passport, driver's license, visa)
- Right to work documentation and expiry tracking
- Worker photo capture and display on badge and profile
- Unique persistent worker ID generated across sites and projects
- Digital signature capture

**Employment & Status Management**
- Employment type classification: employee, contractor, labor hire, subcontractor, volunteer, apprentice
- Current status: active, inactive, suspended, mobilized, demobilized, on leave
- Role and position assignment with effective dates (history of all roles held)
- Department, work group, cost centre, and team assignment
- Reporting manager and supervisor linkage
- Multi-site assignment (one worker assigned across multiple projects simultaneously)
- Contract start date, end date, and tenure tracking
- Re-hire history with prior record access

**Emergency & Contact Information**
- Primary and secondary emergency contacts with relationship, mobile, email
- Blood group, known critical allergies, and disclosed medical conditions (for emergency responder access only)
- Personal physician details
- Next of kin details with preferred notification method

**Document Attachments**
- Attach supporting documents directly to a worker record (employment contract, ID documents, induction certificates)
- Document categorization, expiry date assignment, and version control
- View, download, and replace attached documents with reason logging

**Onboarding Checklists**
- Configurable onboarding checklist templates per employment type or site
- Step-by-step progress tracking with percentage completion
- Assigned task ownership (HR, supervisor, worker each have their own steps)
- Digital acknowledgement of completion per step and overall

**Custom Fields & Extensibility**
- Configurable custom data fields per worker type (text, date, dropdown, boolean, lookup)
- Field-level visibility controls: admin-only, manager-visible, or worker self-service
- Industry-specific fields (e.g. union membership number, trade classification, shift preference)

**Audit Trail & Data Governance**
- Immutable change log on every field: who changed what, when, and from what previous value
- Status change log with mandatory reason codes
- GDPR and Privacy Act compliant data retention rules
- Configurable data retention periods per data category

**Bulk Operations**
- CSV/Excel import for bulk worker creation and updates
- Bulk status changes (mobilize or demobilize a group simultaneously)
- Export worker data in PDF, Excel, or CSV
- Advanced search with saved filter views by role, site, status, cert expiry, contractor, and more

## Module 2 — Competency, Certification & Licensing Management

Manages every formal qualification, license, and competency a worker holds — tracking currency, validity, and fit against the requirements of their role and site.

**Certification Register**
- Centralized register of all certificates and licenses per worker
- Pre-built certificate type library covering common trade and safety tickets: white card, first aid, forklift, confined space entry, electrical licenses, working at heights, crane operations, RIIWHS units, heavy vehicle, explosive handling, SCBA, asbestos removal, and more
- Fully configurable custom certificate types
- Certificate number, issuing authority, issue date, and expiry date per record
- Certificate evidence upload (photo or scan of physical certificate)
- Verification status: pending, verified, failed verification
- Optional integration with external government licensing registers for automated verification

**Expiry Management**
- Configurable multi-stage warning windows (e.g. alerts at 90, 60, 30, 14, 7 days before expiry)
- Auto-escalation if a certificate is not renewed within a grace period
- Expired certificate automatically triggers a site access restriction or role restriction (configurable as hard block or warning-only)
- Grace period settings for certifications where brief post-expiry operation is permitted under a waiver
- Renewal workflow: system notifies worker → worker uploads renewed certificate → manager or HR approves → record updated

**Competency Frameworks**
- Multi-level competency frameworks: novice, competent, proficient, expert (or custom levels)
- Site or project-specific competency assessment (a worker assessed as competent at one site may not yet be assessed at another)
- Assessor assignment and sign-off workflow
- Competency reassessment scheduling and tracking
- Industry-specific competency matrices for mining, construction, oil and gas, utilities, healthcare, and logistics

**Role-to-Certification Mapping**
- Define mandatory and preferred certifications for each role, position, or task type
- Site-specific additional certification requirements layered on top of base role requirements
- Mandatory vs. recommended vs. optional classification per requirement
- Automatic compliance gap identification when a worker is assigned to a role

**Competency Gap Analysis**
- Live dashboard of workers below full certification compliance for their current role
- Filterable by site, contractor, role, department, and time horizon
- Gap report exported to Excel or PDF
- Automated notification to supervisors and safety officers when a gap is identified

**Audit & History**
- Full version history per certificate: original issue, renewals, lapsed periods, cancellations
- Who uploaded the evidence, who verified it, and when it was accepted or rejected
- Exportable certificate register for regulatory inspection

## Module 3 — Induction & Training Management

Ensures every worker completes required inductions before accessing a site, manages ongoing training records, and delivers digital learning — all tracked and auditable.

**Induction Management**
- Site-specific induction workflows with configurable content and steps per project or site type
- Company induction (corporate policies, safety culture, legislative obligations)
- Site induction (site-specific hazards, emergency procedures, site rules, site layout)
- Task or zone inductions (specific areas or high-risk tasks require an additional targeted induction before access is granted)
- Induction delivery methods: online module, face-to-face attendance, video with quiz, digital acknowledgement
- Face-to-face induction attendance register (facilitator marks attendees; digital sign-off per attendee)
- Induction validity periods with auto-expiry (e.g. site induction valid 12 months; expires after long absence)
- Re-induction triggers: expiry, site rule change, incident involvement, return from extended leave

**Online Learning & e-Learning Builder**
- Built-in content builder: drag-and-drop blocks (text, video, PDF embed, images, links)
- SCORM 1.2 and SCORM 2004 content import for third-party e-learning material
- Embedded assessment within modules
- Configurable pass mark per module
- Multiple-attempts settings (unlimited or capped)
- Learning pathways and curriculum builder with prerequisite logic (cannot start module B until module A is passed and a pass mark is achieved)
- CPD point allocation per module and CPD tracking register per worker
- Automated certificate of completion generation on passing

**Assessment Engine**
- Question types: multiple choice, true/false, yes/no, short-answer, image-based, drag-and-drop ordering
- Question bank with randomized question draw to prevent answer copying
- Competency-based assessment scoring
- Immediate feedback on incorrect answers (with or without correct answer reveal, configurable)
- Supervisor-assessed competency sign-off workflow

**Training Matrix**
- Define training requirements per role, site, department, task type, or risk profile
- Worker-level training matrix: required vs. completed vs. outstanding vs. expired, per item
- Workforce-level training compliance dashboard: % compliant by site, role, or contractor
- Training gap report with export capability

**Scheduling & Delivery**
- Training calendar with scheduled face-to-face sessions
- Facilitator and trainer assignment
- Room or location assignment
- Worker self-enroll or manager-assigned enrollment
- Attendance confirmation, no-show recording, and make-up session tracking
- Post-session feedback survey

**Training Record Management**
- Lifetime training history per worker
- Manual entry of external training (off-site courses, university qualifications, professional development)
- Toolbox talk records: topic, facilitator, date, attendees, duration, digital sign-off per attendee
- Training provider and vendor register
- Downloadable and printable training certificates

## Module 4 — Contractor & Subcontractor Management

Manages the complete lifecycle of every external business working on site — from pre-qualification and document compliance through to worker onboarding, active engagement, and performance management.

**Contractor Company Profiles**
- Legal entity register: company name, trading name, ABN/ACN or equivalent, registered address
- Insurance details: type, insurer, policy number, coverage amount, expiry date
- Parent-child contractor hierarchy: contractor → subcontractor → sub-subcontractor, with unlimited nesting depth
- Contractor category and trade type classification
- Contractor status: pending pre-qual, pre-qualified, approved, conditionally approved, suspended, blacklisted
- Account manager and site safety manager assignment per contractor

**Pre-Qualification**
- Configurable pre-qualification questionnaire by contractor category or trade type
- Required document submission: public liability insurance, workers' compensation, professional indemnity, trade licenses, safety management plan, OH&S policy, SWMS register, environmental plan
- Document expiry tracking on all pre-qual documents with automatic alerts before expiry
- Auto-suspension of contractor access when a required document lapses
- Pre-qualification scoring and weighting engine
- Multi-party approval workflow: operations, safety, procurement, legal
- Annual or contract-renewal-triggered re-qualification
- Pre-qualification expiry tracking and re-qualification scheduling

**Contract & Scope Management**
- Contract record per engagement: scope of work, linked site or project, start and end dates
- Contract renewal tracking and alerts
- Work order and purchase order linkage
- Scheduled contract performance reviews

**Contractor Worker Management**
- All workers under a contractor must have individual worker profiles in the system
- Contractor workers subject to the same certification, induction, health, and training requirements as direct employees
- Mass upload of contractor workers during mobilization events
- Contractor manager view limited to their company's workers only (privacy segmentation)

**Contractor Safety Requirements**
- SWMS (Safe Work Method Statement) submission and approval workflow per contractor per task type
- JSA and risk assessment submission register
- Contractor plant and equipment register with inspection records
- Required inductions defined per contractor type
- Incident reporting obligation defined per contractor

**Contractor Self-Service Portal**
- Dedicated login for contractor administrators
- Contractor uploads their own compliance documents and manages their own worker profiles
- Real-time compliance status dashboard visible to contractor manager
- Notification when their workers' certifications or company documents approach expiry
- Submit pre-qualification documents directly via portal, routed to client's approval workflow

**Performance Management**
- Performance scoring criteria: safety behavior, quality, schedule adherence, conduct, incident rate
- Periodic performance review records with scores per criteria
- Non-conformance and corrective action records linked to contractor
- Contractor performance trend and ranking reporting

## Module 5 — Visitor Management

Controls the entry, movement, and departure of all non-workers — clients, auditors, government inspectors, delivery personnel — ensuring site safety obligations extend to everyone on site.

**Pre-Registration**
- Host employee pre-registers visitor: name, organization, purpose, expected arrival and departure, visitor type
- Automated email confirmation and QR code sent to visitor before arrival
- Visitor approval workflow for sites requiring advance authorization (e.g. government or media visits)
- Visitor blacklisting and flagging for previous safety incidents or banned individuals

**Sign-In & Sign-Out**
- Digital sign-in kiosk at site entry (tablet or touchscreen)
- QR code pre-registration sign-in from visitor's mobile
- Host notification on visitor arrival via SMS, email, or push notification
- Vehicle registration capture
- Manual sign-in override for system failures
- Automatic sign-out at a configurable time if visitor has not manually signed out
- Sign-out confirmation and actual departure time capture

**Visitor Type Management**
- Configurable visitor categories: client, auditor, regulatory inspector, delivery driver, contractor visitor, media, emergency services, visitor companion
- Different check-in flows, induction requirements, and access zones per visitor type
- Minimal-access flow for delivery-only visitors (gatehouse only, no site entry)

**Visitor Inductions**
- Short digital visitor induction on the sign-in kiosk covering emergency procedures, hazards, site rules, and prohibited behaviors
- Mandatory completion before entry is permitted
- Digital acknowledgement and signature capture
- Induction bypass option for pre-approved recurring visitors with audit log

**Visitor Badges**
- Auto-generated visitor badge on sign-in: name, photo, host, expiry time, access level
- Color-coded by visitor type
- Optional printed physical badge at kiosk
- Badge expiry time enforcement

**Emergency & Evacuation**
- Real-time visitor register included in the site muster board
- Visitor names automatically added to the emergency evacuation list
- Mass sign-out all visitors with one action in an emergency
- Visitor contact details accessible for emergency notification

**History & Reporting**
- Full visit history per individual with search by name, organization, or date
- Visit frequency patterns and recurring visitor identification
- Non-arrival and late departure exception reporting
- Export visitor logs per site and period for audit

**Form Signing at Entry**
- Configurable forms presented at sign-in: NDA, health declaration, photography consent, site rules acknowledgement
- Digital signature capture stored against the visit record

## Module 6 — Site Access Control & Mobilization

Manages who is authorized to be on site at any given moment — enforcing compliance at the point of entry, providing real-time site occupancy, and managing formal mobilization and demobilization workflows.

**Site Register**
- Real-time list of every person currently on site: name, role, company, check-in time, current zone
- Total headcount counter per site and per zone
- Configurable maximum occupancy limits with alerts when thresholds are reached
- Filterable by contractor, role, employment type, and compliance status

**Check-In / Check-Out Methods**
- QR code scanning via mobile app or site entry kiosk
- NFC badge tap at turnstile or gate
- Geofence-based automatic check-in and check-out via GPS on mobile app
- Biometric check-in at high-security sites (fingerprint or facial recognition)
- Manual check-in by site administrator (override for system failures)
- Vehicle-based check-in (crew arriving together linked to one vehicle registration)
- Supervisor batch check-in for crews without individual devices

**Compliance Gate**
- System executes a real-time compliance check at the point of every check-in
- Check covers: certification currency, induction completion, medical fitness status, PPE issue status, fatigue score
- Non-compliant workers are either hard-blocked or flagged with a warning (configurable per compliance category)
- Supervisor and safety officer notified instantly of a blocked or flagged access attempt
- Exemption and override workflow: a nominated manager can grant temporary access with a mandatory reason code, time limit, and full audit trail

**Mobilization & Demobilization Workflows**
- Formal mobilization workflow: manager initiates, system validates all compliance requirements (certs, inductions, medical clearance, PPE), issues a mobilization approval
- Pre-mobilization checklist per site with all conditions visible before approval is granted
- Demobilization workflow: records date, reason, supervisor sign-off, and removes worker from active site register
- Return-to-site re-validation after absences exceeding a configurable threshold (e.g. 30+ days away triggers re-induction or medical review)

**Multi-Site Management**
- One worker profile active across multiple sites simultaneously
- Site-specific compliance requirements applied independently per site
- Worker's active site status updates as they mobilize and demobilize

**Site Zones & Areas**
- Define sub-zones within a site: blast zones, high-voltage areas, confined spaces, clean rooms, restricted areas
- Additional certifications or inductions required per zone, checked at zone entry
- Zone-level occupancy tracking
- Zone-specific hazard and emergency procedure documentation accessible in-app

**Lone Worker Monitoring**
- Registration of workers operating without direct supervision
- Configurable mandatory check-in frequency (e.g. every 30 minutes)
- Missed check-in alerts escalated to supervisor then safety officer
- SOS emergency button in mobile app with GPS location transmission
- Location trail for audit purposes (with worker consent and privacy controls)

**Emergency Mustering**
- One-click emergency activation by authorized users
- Real-time muster board: confirmed safe vs. outstanding, updated live as workers check in
- Digital muster check-in at assembly point via app scan or kiosk
- Visitor and contractor workers included in the muster list
- Warden accountability tracking
- Post-muster report: time to full accountability, any unaccounted persons, timeline of events

**Access Logs & History**
- Full log of every site entry and exit per worker across all sites and all time
- Anomaly detection: checked in but no check-out after a threshold duration
- Duration on site per session and cumulative per period
- Cross-site movement tracking (time leaving Site A, time arriving at Site B)

## Module 7 — Rostering & Scheduling

Ensures every shift is staffed with the correct number of qualified, fit, and compliant workers — acting simultaneously as an operational planning tool and a frontline safety control.

**Shift & Roster Creation**
- Shift template library: day shift, night shift, afternoon shift, rotating roster, 4-on-4-off, FIFO, DIDO, 8-hour and 12-hour cycles
- Drag-and-drop roster builder with calendar and list views
- Copy and paste shifts or entire roster weeks
- Clone and rollover existing roster patterns across periods
- Bulk shift generation: generate six months of rosters for 200 workers in a single operation

**Compliance-Aware Scheduling**
- System cross-checks each worker's certifications, inductions, medical fitness, and fatigue score before allowing them to be placed on a shift
- Visual alert if a worker's certification will expire before a scheduled shift begins
- Highlight scheduling conflicts: double-booking, insufficient rest between shifts, approaching fatigue risk limits
- Minimum manning rules enforcement: cannot publish a roster that leaves a mandatory minimum below threshold (e.g. at least two first aiders must be rostered on every shift)
- Safety-critical role coverage: roster cannot be published if a defined safety-critical role is unstaffed

**Role-Based & Demand-Based Scheduling**
- Schedule by role type (number of electricians required, number of crane operators required)
- Coverage requirements per role per shift
- Auto-suggest eligible workers for open shifts based on role, certifications, and availability
- Compliance filter on worker suggestions (only surfacing compliant, available workers)

**Availability & Leave Management**
- Worker availability preferences and blocked-out dates
- Leave request submission, manager approval, and automatic roster reflection
- Leave types: annual, sick, RDO, compassionate, long service, LWOP
- Automatic back-fill suggestions when leave is approved
- Leave balance integration with payroll

**Shift Swaps & Open Shifts**
- Worker-initiated swap requests via mobile app
- Compliance validation of the swap partner before the swap is permitted
- Manager approval workflow
- Open shift posting: manager posts unfilled shift; eligible workers can claim it via app

**Roster Publishing & Communication**
- Draft → reviewed → published workflow
- Push notification, SMS, and email to workers on roster publication
- Roster change notifications with reason when a published roster is edited
- Roster accessible to workers via mobile app in their schedule view

**Hours & Overtime Rules**
- Configurable maximum hours per day, week, and rolling fortnight by jurisdiction or award
- Overtime threshold alerts to managers during the scheduling process
- Overtime approval workflow (pre-approval or post-event with reason)
- Cumulative hours tracking per roster period

**On-Call Roster Management**
- On-call rotation schedule for after-hours emergency response
- Callout recording: who was contacted, when, response time, outcome
- On-call hours tracked separately for payroll

## Module 8 — Time & Attendance

Captures when workers start and finish, manages timesheets, monitors deviations, and feeds payroll — with all data GPS-stamped and connected to the compliance record.

**Time Capture Methods**
- Mobile app clock-in and clock-out with GPS timestamp
- QR code scanning at site entry and exit points
- Biometric clock-in: fingerprint or facial recognition at kiosk
- Supervisor batch clock-in for crews without individual devices
- Geofence-based automatic clock-in when worker enters or exits a defined site boundary
- Manual time entry with mandatory reason and supervisor approval

**Timesheet Management**
- Digital timesheets per worker per pay period with shift start, end, break times, and task/project codes
- Worker submits → supervisor approves → payroll exports workflow
- Timesheet editing with mandatory reason logging for any post-clock change
- Bulk approval for supervisors managing large crews
- Timesheet locking after the approval window closes
- Disputed timesheet resolution workflow

**Shift Deviation Alerts**
- Late start: worker has not checked in within the configurable tolerance after shift start
- Early departure: worker checks out before scheduled shift end
- Missed shift: no check-in at all for a scheduled shift
- Overtime alert: worker approaching or exceeding maximum hours threshold
- Unscheduled attendance: worker checks in on a day they are not rostered

**Absence Management**
- Unplanned absence recording with absence reason codes
- Absence pattern detection: frequency analysis by day of week, following leave periods, following incidents
- Return-to-work interview prompt triggered after absences exceeding a defined duration
- Attendance trend reporting per worker and team

**Overtime & Additional Hours**
- Automatic overtime classification based on award or enterprise agreement rules
- Penalty rate triggers: weekends, public holidays, shift differentials
- Overtime approval workflow pre-event or post-event with audit trail
- Cumulative overtime tracking per pay period and rolling 12 weeks

**Payroll Integration**
- Scheduled automated timesheet export to payroll systems (Xero, MYOB, SAP, Workday, ADP, Keypay, Micropay, Employment Hero)
- Cost centre and project code allocation for worked hours
- Leave balance sync
- Award interpretation engine for applicable pay rates

**Hours Compliance Reporting**
- Hours worked vs. regulatory maximum hours per worker
- Total hours per contractor for invoicing purposes
- Weekly and monthly hours summary reports by site, role, and contractor

## Module 9 — Fatigue Risk Management

Actively monitors work patterns against validated fatigue models to identify risk before it contributes to a safety incident — essential in high-consequence industries including mining, transport, healthcare, and oil and gas.

**Hours Monitoring**
- Real-time tracking of hours worked in the current shift, current day, current week, and rolling 28-day window
- Travel time inclusion in fatigue calculations for FIFO and DIDO workers
- Night shift, early morning start, and split shift fatigue multipliers applied automatically
- Time-of-day weighting (2:00 AM–6:00 AM window carries higher fatigue loading than equivalent hours in the day)

**Fatigue Risk Scoring**
- Biomathematical fatigue model integration: FAID (Fatigue Audit InterDyne), or equivalent validated model
- Fatigue score calculated per worker from shift history, sleep opportunity windows, and circadian phase
- Risk band categorization: low, moderate, high, extreme
- Real-time alert when a worker's score crosses into high or extreme risk territory
- Fatigue score visible on the site register for active workers

**Regulatory Hours Limits**
- Configurable hours-of-service rules per industry and jurisdiction: mining regulations, transport hours-of-service, healthcare safe working hours, aviation, rail
- Break interval enforcement: configurable maximum continuous work duration before a mandatory break is required
- Minimum rest period enforcement: minimum hours off between shifts (e.g. 10-hour minimum break)
- Consecutive days worked limit with automated alert

**Roster Pre-Screening for Fatigue**
- Before a roster is published, the system runs a fatigue audit across all scheduled workers
- Flag any planned shifts that would push a worker into a high or extreme fatigue risk band
- Manager must acknowledge or adjust flagged shifts before publication
- Roster-level fatigue risk summary report

**Pre-Shift Fatigue Assessments**
- Digital fatigue self-assessment completed by worker at shift start: sleep hours, perceived fatigue, alertness rating
- Supervisor fatigue observation checklist: observable signs of fatigue assessed by the direct supervisor
- Objective fatigue test integration: reaction time or psychomotor vigilance task
- Failed assessment triggers a mandatory supervisor review and potential stand-down with documented outcome

**Fatigue Waivers**
- Formal waiver request for operational necessity where hours limits must be exceeded
- Multi-party approval: operations, safety, and senior management sign-off
- Waiver frequency limits per worker per period
- Full documentation retained for regulatory audit

**Reporting**
- Fatigue score trend per worker over time
- Workforce-level fatigue heatmap by site, shift, and time period
- Near-miss and incident correlation with fatigue scores
- High-fatigue event frequency by worker, role, and contractor

## Module 10 — Health & Medical Fitness Management

Manages every health obligation placed on workers — pre-employment medicals, ongoing surveillance, fitness-for-work status, rehabilitation — with strict privacy controls throughout.

**Fitness-for-Work Status**
- Fitness status classification per worker: Fit / Fit with Restrictions / Temporarily Unfit / Permanently Unfit
- Restriction detail: specific tasks, equipment, or exposures the worker cannot perform (e.g. no confined space entry, no heavy lifting, no driving, audiometric-restricted environment)
- Fitness status change history with reason codes and effective dates
- Supervisor notified of a status change with only the outcome (fit/unfit/restricted) — the underlying medical details remain private

**Pre-Employment Medicals**
- Medical requirement triggered automatically at the start of the onboarding workflow
- Assignment to approved occupational health service providers
- Medical outcome recorded: pass, conditional pass, fail
- Medical certificate upload and attachment to the worker profile
- Medical type and frequency defined per role risk profile (e.g. annual for high-risk, triennial for low-risk)

**Periodic Health Surveillance**
- Surveillance schedules defined by occupational hazard exposure:
  - Audiometry for noise-exposed workers
  - Spirometry and lung function testing for dust and chemical exposure
  - Biological monitoring for workers exposed to defined hazardous substances
  - Skin, dermatology, and ophthalmology checks for relevant exposure types
  - Vision testing for roles with defined visual acuity requirements
- Automatic surveillance scheduling triggered by role, work area hazard profile, and elapsed time since last test
- Overdue surveillance alerts to worker, supervisor, and occupational health team

**Drug & Alcohol Testing Records**
- Test record management: date, test type (urine, breath, oral fluid), result, tester name, reason code
- Test reason codes: pre-employment, random, post-incident, for-cause, return-to-work
- Positive result workflow: immediate stand-down, EAP referral, HR and safety escalation, chain-of-custody documentation
- Testing frequency configuration by worker category and risk classification
- Aggregate reporting for regulatory compliance

**Return to Work & Rehabilitation**
- RTW plan creation and management with graduated return schedule
- Restricted duties tracking and supervisor attestation at each stage
- Milestone check-ins and plan reviews linked to treating practitioner recommendations
- Workers' compensation claim linkage
- Occupational therapist and rehabilitation provider management

**Medical Provider Management**
- Approved occupational health provider register with contact details, service types, and location
- Referral and booking management
- Provider portal for direct results submission into the platform
- Invoice and cost tracking per provider

**Occupational Exposure Management**
- Occupational hazard exposure profiles assigned to roles and work areas
- Cumulative exposure tracking: noise dose, chemical exposure hours, UV exposure
- Exposure thresholds defined with automated surveillance triggers when thresholds are approached
- Exposure register reports for regulatory submission

**Privacy & Access Controls**
- Medical records stored in a segregated, encrypted data partition
- Granular role-based access: only nominated occupational health and HR users can view full medical records
- Supervisors see only the fitness classification — not the clinical detail behind it
- GDPR and Privacy Act compliant consent, retention, and erasure workflows
- Audit log of every access to a medical record

**Emergency Health Information**
- Worker-disclosed emergency health card: blood group, critical allergies, relevant conditions, current medications
- Accessible to designated first aiders in an emergency (not to supervisors generally)
- Printable and offline-accessible emergency card format

## Module 11 — PPE Management

Tracks what PPE is required per role and hazard, confirms it has been issued to each worker, and manages condition, expiry, and replacement — turning PPE compliance from a paperwork exercise into an active mobilization gate.

**PPE Requirement Matrix**
- Define required PPE by role, task type, work area, and hazard type
- PPE types: hard hat, steel-capped boots, hi-vis vest, safety glasses, face shield, hearing protection, gloves, harness and lanyard, SCBA, FR (flame-resistant) clothing, chemical-resistant suit, cut-resistant gloves, and fully configurable custom types
- Mandatory vs. recommended vs. optional classification
- PPE substitution rules where equivalent items are accepted
- Site-specific and zone-specific PPE requirements layered on top of base role requirements

**PPE Issuance**
- Digital issuance record per worker per item: date, item type, brand and model, size, serial number (for serialized assets), condition on issue
- Worker digital sign-off confirming receipt
- Batch issuance workflow for onboarding cohorts
- Full issuance history per worker

**PPE Inventory Management**
- Inventory register at each site and warehouse
- Low stock alerts with configurable reorder threshold
- Supplier and procurement tracking
- Cost tracking per issue and per worker per period

**Expiry & Service Tracking**
- Expiry dates for time-limited PPE: safety helmets, gas detection equipment, SCBA cylinders, respiratory cartridges
- Service interval tracking for inspectable or reusable items: fall arrest equipment, SCBA sets, harnesses
- Pre-expiry and pre-service-due alerts
- Equipment quarantine workflow when an item fails inspection, is damaged, or reaches expiry

**Compliance Gate at Mobilization**
- PPE compliance check integrated into the pre-mobilization workflow
- Worker cannot be mobilized to a site if required PPE has not been issued or has expired
- PPE status visible on the worker compliance dashboard

**Inspection & Condition Records**
- Inspection record per serialized item: date, inspector, result, findings
- Photo attachment for damage documentation
- Failed inspection triggers quarantine status and a replacement workflow
- Periodic inspection schedule management per item type

**Worker Self-Service**
- Workers view their current PPE, condition, and any items due for replacement via the mobile app
- PPE replacement request submission with reason
- Workers receive notifications when their issued PPE is approaching service or expiry

## Module 12 — Incident & Near-Miss Integration

Connects every safety event to the workers involved — enabling root cause analysis, trend identification, and corrective action workflows that tie back directly to the workforce management record.

**Incident Record Linkage**
- Every incident or near-miss is linked to the worker profiles of those involved: injured party, witnesses, supervisors on duty, persons in charge of the area
- Incident type: workplace injury, near-miss, property damage, environmental, first aid only, hazard observation, dangerous occurrence
- Site, zone, task, and equipment linkage
- Time and shift linkage (connects to rostering and fatigue data automatically)

**Injury & Illness Tracking**
- Injured body part classification
- Injury type: laceration, fracture, crush, sprain/strain, burn, respiratory, noise-induced, occupational illness
- Severity classification: first aid only, medical treatment required, restricted work duty, lost time, permanent impairment, fatality
- Lost time injury tracking with LTIFR, TRIFR, and MTIFR calculation
- Days lost tracking and automatic linkage to the RTW module for rehabilitation management

**Near-Miss & Hazard Observation Reporting**
- Mobile-first near-miss and hazard submission by any worker: description, category, photo, GPS location, risk rating
- Categorization by type and contributing factors
- Corrective action generation from submitted observations
- Action assignment, due date, and completion tracking

**Investigation Management**
- Formal investigation workflow: assigned investigator, investigation due date, preliminary findings, final report
- Root cause analysis tools: 5 Whys, fishbone/Ishikawa, fault tree analysis
- Causal factor tagging linked to workforce data: fatigue, training gap, supervision failure, procedural non-compliance, equipment deficiency
- Corrective and preventive action (CAPA) register with owner, due date, and closure workflow
- Post-investigation lessons-learned distribution workflow

**Workers' Compensation & Regulatory Reporting**
- Workers' comp claim initiation from an incident record with pre-filled form data
- Notifiable incident flag with regulatory notification workflow (WHS Act, RIDDOR, OSHA, as applicable per jurisdiction)
